Discover Aberdeen

Aberdeen, located on the northeastern coast of Scotland, is a city incredibly rich in history, favorable in beauty, and blessed with a vibrant culture. Sometimes called the Granite City because of its thousands of distinctive grey stone buildings, Aberdeen has fused historic Old-World charm with cosmopolitan urban culture. It expresses its rich cultural heritage in the very important landmarks such as Aberdeen Harbour and Marischal College. Ample opportunities abound for the adventurers-sunny beaches, breathtaking scenic coastline, and nearby Royal Deeside. Food is very much a major consideration in Aberdonians' lives. Festivities and events such as art exhibitions and concerts celebrate live music throughout the year. The seafood in this city varies from fast food to a gourmet experience. One warm and welcoming welcome makes any outsider feel right at home no matter what business he might be out for.

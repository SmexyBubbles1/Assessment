Explore Aberdeen's Best Attractions

Discover breathtaking sights, thrilling activities, and hidden gems in Aberdeen.

Aberdeen Harbour

Aberdeen Harbour, one of the busiest ports in Scotland, offers both historical and modern maritime experiences. Take a stroll along the waterfront or explore the nearby Maritime Museum.

Learn More
Marischal College

A stunning example of Scottish granite architecture, Marischal College is a magnificent landmark. Explore the architecture and learn about the city's academic heritage.

Learn More
Royal Deeside

For nature lovers, Royal Deeside is the perfect escape. This picturesque area offers scenic hiking trails, breathtaking views, charming villages, and historic castles to explore and enjoy.

Events You Can’t Miss

Light the Blue Festival

The Aberdeen Light the Blue Festival is a vibrant celebration of art and culture. Experience stunning light displays, live performances, and immersive installations that illuminate the city's streets, captivating visitors of all ages.

Learn More
Spectra

Aberdeen Spectra is an awe-inspiring festival of light and sound, where you can explore stunning light installations, captivating projections, and interactive displays that transform the city into a breathtaking visual and sensory experience.
